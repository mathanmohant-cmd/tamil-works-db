Tamil Concordance: A Treasure by Prof. P. Pandiaraja

This project is deeply indebted to Professor P. Pandiaraja, whose creation of the Tamil Concordance stands as a monumental resource. Over more than five decades, he has built a searchable treasure of Tamil words across Sangam and later literature, blending his expertise in mathematics and computers with his passion for Tamil.

His dedication, simplicity, and continuous creative contribution to Tamil long after retirement is an inspiration for all.

His Contribution

  • Five decades of dedication: Meticulous study of Tamil words across Sangam and later literature.
  • Books of precision: His works reflect an insatiable appetite for exactness and nuanced understanding.
  • Mathematics meets language: Introduced statistical and computational methods into Tamil word studies.
  • Interdisciplinary bridge: Blended academic expertise in mathematics, computing, and Tamil culture.

Why It Matters

His work provides researchers with structured concordance, scholars with a foundation for comparative studies, and Tamil speakers everywhere with deeper insights into their language.
